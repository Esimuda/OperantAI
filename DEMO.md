# Operant AI — Live Demo Script

## Setup (do this before judges arrive)
1. Open Operant at https://operant-ai.vercel.app
2. Click Dashboard tab in top right
3. Have Notion database open in another tab
4. Have your email inbox open in another tab

## The Demo (2 minutes)

### Minute 1 — Show the AI
1. Type this in the chat:
   "When someone submits my Typeform, add them to
   my Notion leads database and send them a welcome email"
2. Show how Claude instantly understands and builds the workflow
3. Point out the Live Integration Active badge

### Minute 2 — Show it actually works
1. Click the Dashboard tab
2. Click "⚡ Simulate Live Workflow"
3. Watch the execution feed in real time:
   - Step 1: Notion record created ✓
   - Step 2: Welcome email sent ✓
4. Switch to Notion tab — show the record appeared
5. Switch to email tab — show the welcome email arrived

## Key Lines To Say
- "Most automation tools make you drag and drop nodes manually"
- "Operant converts plain English into live running workflows"
- "Watch this — I'll describe a workflow and it executes in real time"
- "The workflow just ran. Notion updated. Email sent. Zero manual setup."
